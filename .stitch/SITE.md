# NXT//LINK Site Memory

## Site Purpose
Public-facing intelligence website for understanding live market movement, vendors, products, events, and regional activity.

## Page Priorities
- `/` homepage: Explain the product quickly and route users into the best first action.
- `/briefing`: Highest-priority starting point for new users.
- `/intel`: Live evidence and raw signal exploration.
- `/vendors`, `/products`, `/conferences`, `/map`: Deeper action routes after orientation.

## Screen Strategy
1. Brief first.
2. Show evidence second.
3. Route to action third.

## Design Guardrails
- Do not make the homepage read like a generic AI landing page.
- Avoid overly abstract marketing copy.
- Keep the command-center look, but make the product understandable to non-technical users.
- Prefer a few strong sections over many competing sections.
